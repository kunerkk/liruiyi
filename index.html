🏡 Pet Adoption Process & Requirements

Adoption Requirements

  • Valid identity card & permanent address proof
  • Complete home inspection or video house tour
  • Pay adoption fee (covers vaccines, deworming & sterilization)
  • Promise to spay/neuter the pet at the correct age
  • No history of animal abandonment or abuse
  • Stable income & enough living space for pets

Step-by-Step Adoption Guide

1. Submit Application Form: Fill out our online adoption form, tell us your living environment and pet experience.

2. Meet & Greet Session: Visit our shelter, interact with pets to find the most matching companion.

3. Home Environment Check: Staff will conduct video or offline home visit to confirm safety.

4. Sign Adoption Contract: Read and sign the formal adoption agreement, promise lifelong care.

5. Bring Pet Home: Pay adoption fee, receive pet food, toys and medical records.
